About TradeBridge

Revolutionizing global B2B trade through technology and trust

Our Story

Founded in 2023, TradeBridge emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make global B2B trade accessible, transparent, and efficient for businesses of all sizes. Our founders, experienced in international trade and technology, recognized the challenges faced by businesses trying to connect with reliable partners across borders.

Traditional trade processes were fragmented, time-consuming, and risky. Businesses struggled with finding verified suppliers, securing financing, ensuring quality, and managing complex logistics. TradeBridge was born to solve these challenges through an integrated, technology-driven platform.

Today, we serve over 25,000 businesses across 120 countries, facilitating billions of dollars in trade volume annually. Our platform has become the trusted bridge connecting global commerce, enabling businesses to grow beyond borders with confidence.
