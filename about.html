About Flonze

India's leading mobile eco-friendly car detailing service

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Flonze emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize car care in India by making professional detailing services accessible, convenient, and environmentally responsible.

We recognized that busy urban lifestyles left little time for proper vehicle maintenance, while traditional car washes often used harsh chemicals harmful to both vehicles and the environment. This gap inspired us to create a mobile service that brings premium, eco-friendly car detailing directly to our customers' doorsteps.

Starting in Mumbai, we've rapidly expanded across major Indian cities, serving over 10,000 satisfied customers who trust us with their vehicles. Our commitment to quality, convenience, and environmental responsibility has made us the preferred choice for discerning car and bike owners.
