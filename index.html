Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start using your service?

All major platforms - Shopify, Amazon, Flipkart, WooCommerce, Meesho, and more. We can also integrate with any custom platform you use.

We handle everything! Returns, quality checks, restocking, refunds - all automated. You don't need to worry about return management at all.
